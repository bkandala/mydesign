# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include harassment, trolling, insulting comments, and other unprofessional conduct.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.
